30 Days of Families Learning Together

Families will be spending a lot of time together in the days ahead, which can be an opportunity rather than a challenge. NCFL’s guide, "30 Days of Families Learning Together," offers a collection of 30 free family literacy activities designed to make the most of this time. Each day, the guide presents engaging and interactive activities that promote learning and strengthen family bonds. From creative projects to educational games and thought-provoking discussions, these activities turn everyday moments into valuable learning experiences. By following this guide, families can enrich their time together, foster a love of learning, and create lasting memories.

Cultivating Readers

Cultivating readers involves creating an environment that nurtures a love for books and reading from an early age. By providing children with access to a variety of reading materials and modeling enthusiastic reading habits, you lay the foundation for their lifelong literacy. Reading aloud to children not only enhances their language skills but also fosters a love for stories and imagination. Encouraging independent reading and allowing children to choose their own books can further develop their interest and engagement with reading. Regular discussions about books and integrating reading into daily activities make the practice a natural and enjoyable part of life.

Family Time Machine

The Family Time Machine is a dynamic and creative tool designed to transform everyday family moments into opportunities for learning and connection. It encourages families to engage in fun and educational activities that turn routine interactions—like mealtimes, bedtime, and playtime—into enriching experiences. By providing a wide array of activities and prompts, the Family Time Machine inspires families to explore new ideas, learn together, and build stronger bonds. Whether it’s through imaginative play, creative projects, or educational games, the Family Time Machine helps families make the most of their time together, fostering a love of learning and creating lasting memories

Practitioner Toolkit

The Practitioner Toolkit is an essential resource for educators and family literacy instructors working with adult English language learners. This comprehensive toolkit offers a wealth of practical strategies, teaching materials, and support resources designed to enhance instruction and improve learning outcomes. It includes guidance on effective teaching practices, culturally responsive strategies, and tools for assessing and addressing the unique needs of adult learners. By providing valuable insights and practical solutions, the Practitioner Toolkit empowers educators to create engaging, supportive, and effective learning environments, helping adult learners achieve their educational and personal goals
